Puyo Shooter is a creative multiplayer PVP VR
game. Players will become a hippo whose left
hands hold a bow and shoot the opponent. There
are many different types of arrows and protective
props, making the game more unpredictable and
more fun! Explosive arrow will destroy the floor
and create the sense of weightlessness. The heart
arrow can  rebound, the toilet plunger can block
the eye sight and the scattered arrow can give
out 3 arrows together. Each shooting is full of
surprises! Come play with your friends and see
who will win in the end!
